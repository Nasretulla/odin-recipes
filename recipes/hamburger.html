Best Hamburger Ever

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Meanwhile, Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
  3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 4 to 5 minutes per side.
